HR Policy Document - TechCorp Pakistan
Leave Policy: Employees get 20 annual leaves per year
Sick Leave: 10 sick leaves per year
Working Hours: 9am to 6pm Monday to Friday
Salary Payment: Every 1st of the month
Probation Period: 3 months for new employees
Medical Benefits: Company pays 50% of medical bills
Annual Bonus: Given every December based on performance
Remote Work: Allowed 2 days per week
Dress Code: Smart casual Monday to Thursday, Casual Friday
Harassment Policy: Zero tolerance for workplace harassment
Promotion Policy: Based on performance review every 6 months
Training: Company provides free training every quarter
